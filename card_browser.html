Lost In Nature.

Amid the chaos of life, there comes a moment when silence becomes the loudest teacher. Lost in nature’s silence, I felt the world slow down, every rustle of the leaves and every whisper of the breeze carrying a message of peace. The tall trees stood like ancient guardians, while the stillness of the forest wrapped around me like a warm embrace. In that quiet, I realized that silence is not empty—it is full of answers we often fail to hear in the rush of daily life
